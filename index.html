01Controller

MagicBackdrop is a private application. For questions, please contact the app provider.

02What data is processed?

MagicBackdrop processes only the following data:

  • Photos taken with your camera
  • A background image selected from your gallery
  • Your remove.bg API key

No personal data (name, email, location, etc.) is collected or stored.

03Use of the remove.bg API

This app uses the remove.bg service (Kaleido AI GmbH) to automatically remove backgrounds from photos.

When you take a photo, it is transmitted to remove.bg servers for processing. remove.bg processes the image and returns a version with the background removed.

The remove.bg privacy policy applies to this transfer:
remove.bg/privacy

04Local data storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • API key: encrypted (EncryptedSharedPreferences / Keychain)
  • Background image path: in app storage

The app does not send any data to its own servers.

05Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app displays advertisements via Google AdMob (Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4).

A GDPR consent dialog is shown on first launch. Without consent, only non-personalized ads are shown.

Google may process data through advertising. For more information:
policies.google.com/privacy

06Your rights

You can delete your saved settings at any time by uninstalling the app. For processing by remove.bg or Google AdMob, please contact the respective providers directly.
